Video Consultation Terms & Privacy

Important Privacy Notice

Please read this entire document before booking or participating in an online video consultation. By proceeding with an online appointment, you acknowledge and agree to the terms below.

1. Encryption & Security

  • Video consultations use secure HTTPS encryption during transmission
  • Video and audio are NOT end-to-end encrypted
  • The video platform provider (Jitsi) can theoretically access video data
  • We recommend using a private network (not public WiFi) for consultations
  • For highly sensitive matters, in-person visits are more secure

2. Do NOT Share During Video Calls

  • Social Security Numbers or Tax ID numbers
  • Credit card numbers or banking information
  • PIN codes or passwords
  • Driver's license or government ID details
  • Insurance policy details (if not necessary)
  • Any other highly sensitive personal information

If needed: Share sensitive information via secure methods (encrypted email, phone, or in-person)

3. Data Storage & Recording

  • Video consultations are NOT automatically recorded
  • Appointment details are stored in our secure database
  • Recording is only done with explicit consent from both parties
  • Data is retained per Philippines Data Privacy Act (RA 10173)
  • You have the right to request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)

4. Third Party Platform

Online video consultations are powered by Jitsi Meet, an open-source video platform.

  • Jitsi Meet is hosted on a public server (meet.jit.si)
  • Your appointment details are NOT shared with Jitsi
  • Only the video session is processed by Jitsi servers
  • See Jitsi's privacy policy: jitsi.org/about/
  • You are responsible for understanding and accepting Jitsi's terms

5. Your Responsibilities

  • Use a secure, private internet connection
  • Choose a private location where others cannot see/hear your medical consultation
  • Have a stable internet connection and working camera/microphone
  • Clear your browser history after the consultation
  • Notify us immediately if you suspect a breach or unauthorized access
  • Do not record or screenshot the consultation without doctor's consent

6. Doctor's Responsibilities

  • Maintain professional privacy and confidentiality
  • Consult from a private location free from interruptions
  • Use secure internet connection
  • Not record consultations without patient's explicit written consent
  • Comply with all medical professional standards and ethics

7. When to Avoid Video Consultations

In-person visits are recommended for:

  • Physical examinations requiring touch
  • Procedural treatments or injections
  • Complex diagnostic assessments
  • Patients with limited technology access
  • Consultations requiring utmost confidentiality

8. Your Consent

By checking the "Online Video Consultation" checkbox and proceeding with booking, you:

  • Acknowledge you have read this privacy notice
  • Understand the security limitations of video consultations
  • Accept the risks associated with online medical consultations
  • Agree not to hold OkiDoki liable for third-party platform breaches
  • Consent to participate in video consultations on these terms

9. Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about these terms or concerns about privacy:

  • Contact us at: support@okidoki.clinic
  • Or use the contact form on our website
